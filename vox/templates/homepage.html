Empuraan

L2: Empuraan (stylized as L2 E.M.P.U.R.A.A.N - Lucifer 2), also marketed as L2E, is a 2025 Indian Malayalam-language action thriller film directed by Prithviraj Sukumaran and written by Murali Gopy. It was jointly produced by Antony Perumbavoor, Gokulam Gopalan, and A. Subaskaran through Aashirvad Cinemas, Sree Gokulam Movies, and Lyca Productions, respectively. Lyca Productions sold their rights to Sree Gokulam movies at the last minute. The film is both the prequel of sequel of the 2019 Indian film Lucifer and is the second installment in the L Franchise
